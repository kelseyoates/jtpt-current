About

Clients

Jay works with residents of Sharon, Stoughton, and Canton and also travels to Cambridge and Weston to work with individuals at their homes. His clients come from a variety of health backgrounds including Asperger’s/autism, multiple sclerosis, depression, obesity, diabetes, asthma, PTSD, and joint replacements.

Specialties

Jay specializes in corrective exercise (postural/movement imbalances), weight loss, and athletic training.

Background

Jay has a bachelor’s degree in nursing from UMass-Lowell and is certified in personal training through the National Academy of Sports Medicine (NASM). He is the head coach of the Norton High varsity hockey team. He has also coached high school baseball for the last five years.
For ongoing updates from Jay, check out the blog.
